**Trolley-X** is an autonomous, retrofittable follow-me cart for multi-payload
transport — our MSc Robotics dissertation project at Middlesex University Dubai.
It follows its operator hands-free using UWB positioning fused with a Kalman
filter, and stops safely around people and obstacles with a three-zone LiDAR
safety system. This blog tracks the build from a validated ROS 2 simulation to a
working physical prototype.
