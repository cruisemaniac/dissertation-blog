## The project

Trolley-X is an autonomous, retrofittable cart that carries varied payloads,
follows its operator hands-free, and navigates safely around people. It is built
for the PDE4445 Robotics Dissertation Project at Middlesex University Dubai, and
extends a validated ROS 2 Jazzy simulation into a physical prototype.
